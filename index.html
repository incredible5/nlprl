Natural language processing (NLP) studies the mathematical and computational modelling of various aspects of natural language and develops a wide range of technologies and systems to facilitate human-computer interaction as well as human-human interaction.


The last decade has seen a rapid growth in the research and development in the area of Natural Language Processing and Computational Linguistics in India. We already have developed and deployed some language resources, important tools and larger systems, such as Machine Translation for some of the major Indian languages like Hindi, Urdu, Bangla, Marathi, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu etc.


The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT(BHU) was selected by the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology as a node in the National endeavor on Technology Development for Indian Languages. In the NLP Lab of the department research is focused on various sub-areas of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and Information Extraction.
